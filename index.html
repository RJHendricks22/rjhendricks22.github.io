BRICK BREAKER V1.0

A JS game by Ray Hendricks

INSTRUCTIONS

CHOOSE YOUR DIFFICULTY:

Instructions:

  1. Choose the desired difficulty, Easy, Medium or Hard
  2. Mouse over the game window and the paddle will move with your mouse
  3. Keep the ball from falling below the paddle, and break all the blocks to win!
  4. The ball starts moving faster over time, so watch out!
